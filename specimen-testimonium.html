— Specimen This page shows the standard form of the Pilgrim Testimonium issued at the close of an official pilgrim tour. The form below uses placeholder fields where an actual testimonium carries the pilgrim's legal name, the tour route, the issuing Mission, the date, and a unique reference. The watermark and seal are cosmetic; an original document is impressed in red wax. Specimens are not valid for travel and cannot be presented as credentials.
